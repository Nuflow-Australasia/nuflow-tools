Design Parameters
  • Short-term material flexural modulus of 2,500 MPa as provided by Nuflow.
  • Vehicle loading uses SM1600 loads per AS5100.2:2017, distributed per AS/NZS 2566.1.
  • 4 MPa combined soil modulus — host pipe wall must be snug against liner without voids. Deteriorated pipes require project-specific design.
  • MIN and MAX cover figures are measured from FSL to top of pipe.
⚠️ This is a preliminary reference tool only. Any project outside these parameters must have a Liner Design Verification Form completed.

The information below must be carefully reviewed and factored into your application.

Resin usage

The viscosity of resin may be affected by ambient conditions, potentially altering the volume of resin required during impregnation. It is essential to verify all calculations on-site through visual inspection to ensure adequate saturation.

Roller gap variances

Impregnation equipment varies. Factors such as rubber coatings on rollers, positional hysteresis, and roller flex can influence the effective gap. As a result, settings may differ between equipment types. Additionally, cordless and electric drive systems may produce different outcomes.

Testing Plugs

Hydraulic & Air Testing

Hydraulic and air testing is essential for pipes and sewers to ensure their safe and efficient operation. Tests are performed to control cracks, leaks, welding defects and other failures in these pipes and sewers. Diversion of flow during pipeline inspection, cleaning, damage assessment or repair is also a primary role of the testing plug.

Applications

  • Pressure and leakage testing of new installations
  • Flow diversion during inspection or cleaning
  • Isolation of pipeline sections for repair
  • Post-rehabilitation acceptance testing

Available Configurations

  • Single-end plug — for pipe termination isolation
  • Through-plug — for mid-line flow diversion
  • Bypass equipped — with integrated bypass port
  • Custom sizes and pressure ratings available on request
